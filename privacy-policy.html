1. Introduction

Nomii (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use the Nomii mobile application, web portal, and related services (“Services”).

2. Information We Collect

Information Provided by You

  • Account identifiers such as email address, phone number, or Apple ID (appAccountToken).
  • Profile data including display name, avatar, preferred language, and conversation preferences.
  • Uploaded media (images, voice notes, chat content) that power AI persona generation.
  • Billing metadata associated with purchases processed via Apple or other payment partners.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (model, OS version, locale) and basic diagnostics for crash analytics.
  • Usage data such as session events, feature interactions, and aggregated performance metrics.
  • Subscription status and server-side telemetry required to deliver premium benefits.

3. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate your account, prevent fraud, and maintain service availability.
  • Generate AI personas, deliver chat experiences, and store artwork generated on your behalf.
  • Process purchases, manage subscription entitlements, and deliver RICE virtual currency.
  • Provide customer support, troubleshoot issues, and improve our product roadmap.
  • Comply with legal obligations, enforce our Terms of Service, and respond to lawful requests.

4. How We Share Information

  • Service providers that host infrastructure, deliver notifications, or process email.
  • Payment processors such as Apple to validate in-app purchases and detect abuse.
  • Analytics or AI partners strictly limited to delivering Nomii features; data is pseudonymized whenever possible.
  • Law enforcement or regulators when required by applicable law or to protect our users.

We do not sell personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking, and we do not share device identifiers with ad networks.

6. International Transfers

Nomii operates globally. Your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our infrastructure or vendors are located. We implement contractual and technical safeguards consistent with applicable regulations.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Core account data (profile, chats, generated content, avatars, tokens) is deleted when you complete in-app account deletion. Transaction records may be kept where law requires. Diagnostics and analytics logs are stored for a limited period to secure and improve the service.

8. Account Deletion

Delete your account directly in the app: Profile > Account actions > Delete account. The request is processed immediately in-app; no email or external website is required.

  • What happens: we delete your profile, chat history, generated media, avatars, and active tokens, and sign you out.
  • Sign in with Apple: we revoke the linked authorization token on our servers when deletion is completed.
  • Subscriptions: deleting your account does not cancel an Apple subscription. Manage renewals in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.
  • Legal exceptions: we may retain limited records if required by law or for fraud prevention.

9. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, correct, or delete your profile data via in-app settings or by contacting us.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing messages at any time using unsubscribe links or device settings.
  • Request export of your data in a portable format.
  • Disable personalized analytics by adjusting mobile OS privacy controls.

For detailed instructions, visit our Privacy Choices Center.

10. Children's Privacy

Nomii is not directed to children under the age specified by local regulations (13 in the U.S., 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we collected such data, contact us to delete it.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" above and notify users via in-app messaging or email where required.
