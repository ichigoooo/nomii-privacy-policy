Nomii Privacy Choices Center

Manage how your personal data is used across Nomii experiences.

Access or Correct Your Data

Open the Nomii app → Profile > Settings > Privacy to review or edit your profile, avatar, default languages, and stored persona information. If you cannot access the app, contact privacy@nomii.app with the email/phone tied to your account.

Download or Transfer Data

Request a portable export of your conversations, characters, and purchase history by emailing data-export@nomii.app. We will verify your identity and send a secure download link within 30 days, in accordance with GDPR/CCPA requirements.

Delete Your Account

  • In-app: Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  • Email: account-removal@nomii.app with subject “Account Deletion”.

We will remove personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required for billing or legal obligations.

Marketing Preferences

  • Toggle marketing notifications inside the app under Settings → Notifications.
  • Use the “unsubscribe” link at the bottom of any Nomii marketing email.
  • Control push notifications via iOS Settings → Nomii → Notifications.

Analytics & Personalization Controls

  • Disable personalized ads by using iOS Tracking permissions (Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking).
  • Restrict background data collection via iOS Settings → Nomii → Allow Nomii to Access.
  • Within the Nomii app, toggle “Use conversation history to improve AI” under Settings → Privacy.
